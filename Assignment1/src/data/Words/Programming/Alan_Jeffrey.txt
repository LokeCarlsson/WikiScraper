Archive</a></div>/n</td>/n</tr>/n</table>/n<p><b>Alan Samuel Jeffrey</b> (born 18 October 1963 in <a href="/wiki/Derry" title="Derry">Derry</a>, <a href="/wiki/Northern_Ireland" title="Northern Ireland">Northern Ireland</a>)<sup id="cite_ref-CAP_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-CAP-1">[1]</a></sup> is a former <a href="/wiki/Ireland_cricket_team" title="Ireland cricket team">Irish cricketer</a>. A left-handed <a href="/wiki/Batsman" class="mw-redirect" title="Batsman">batsman</a> and left-arm <a href="/wiki/Fast_bowling" title="Fast bowling">medium pace</a> <a href="/wiki/Bowler_(cricket)" class="mw-redirect" title="Bowler (cricket)">bowler</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-CAP_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-CAP-1">[1]</a></sup> he played eleven times for the <a href="/wiki/Ireland_cricket_team" title="Ireland cricket team">Ireland cricket team</a> between 1984 and 1989<sup id="cite_ref-CESZP_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-CESZP-2">[2]</a></sup> including one <a href="/wiki/First-class_cricket" title="First-class cricket">first-class</a> match<sup id="cite_ref-FCM_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FCM-3">[3]</a></sup> and three <a href="/wiki/List_A_cricket" title="List A cricket">List A</a> matches.<sup id="cite_ref-LAM_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-LAM-4">[4]</a></sup></p>/n<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Playing_career">Playing career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Alan_Jeffrey&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Playing career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>/n<p>Jeffrey made his debut for Ireland against the <a href="/wiki/Marylebone_Cricket_Club" title="Marylebone Cricket Club">MCC</a> in June 1984. He played against the <a href="/wiki/West_Indies_cricket_team" title="West Indies cricket team">West Indies</a> later that month<sup id="cite_ref-CESZP_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-CESZP-2">[2]</a></sup> and made his List A debut the following month against <a href="/wiki/Surrey_County_Cricket_Club" title="Surrey County Cricket Club">Surrey</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Friends_Provident_Trophy" title="Friends Provident Trophy">NatWest Trophy</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-LAM_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-LAM-4">[4]</a></sup> He played against <a href="/wiki/Wales_national_cricket_team" title="Wales national cricket team">Wales</a> eleven days later, but did not play for Ireland again for almost three years.<sup id="cite_ref-CESZP_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-CESZP-2">[2]</a></sup></p>/n<p>He returned to the Irish side for a match against <a href="/wiki/Northamptonshire_County_Cricket_Club" title="Northamptonshire County Cricket Club">Northamptonshire</a> in June 1987, before playing six times in 1989.<sup id="cite_ref-CESZP_2-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-CESZP-2">[2]</a></sup> This included his only first-class game, against <a href="/wiki/Scotland_national_cricket_team" title="Scotland national cricket team">Scotland</a><sup id="cite_ref-FCM_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FCM-3">[3]</a></sup> and his final List A game against <a href="/wiki/Derbyshire_County_Cricket_Club" title="Derbyshire County Cricket Club">Derbyshire</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-LAM_4-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-LAM-4">[4]</a></sup> His final game for Ireland was against Wales.<sup id="cite_ref-CESZP_2-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-CESZP-2">[2]</a></sup></p>/n<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Statistics">Statistics</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Alan_Jeffrey&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Statistics">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>/n<p>In all matches for Ireland, he scored 91 <a href="/wiki/Run_(cricket)" title="Run (cricket)">runs</a> at an <a href="/wiki/Batting_average#Cricket" title="Batting average">average</a> of 22.75 with a top score of 28 against Wales in July 1984. He took 15 <a href="/wiki/Wicket#Dismissing_a_batsman" title="Wicket">wickets</a> at an <a href="/wiki/Bowling_average" title="Bowling average">average</a> of 39.73, with best <a href="/wiki/Innings#Cricket" title="Innings">innings</a> bowling figures of 3/22 against Scotland in July 1989.<sup id="cite_ref-CESZP_2-5" class="reference"><a
