Morgan Kaufmann's audience includes the research and development communities, information technology (IS/IT) managers, and students in professional degree programs.</p>/n<p>The company was founded in 1984 by publishers Michael B. Morgan and William Kaufmann and computer scientist <a href="/wiki/Nils_Nilsson_(researcher)" class="mw-redirect" title="Nils Nilsson (researcher)">Nils Nilsson</a>. It was held privately until 1998, when it was acquired by <a href="/wiki/Harcourt_General" class="mw-redirect" title="Harcourt General">Harcourt General</a> and became an <a href="/wiki/Imprint_(trade_name)" title="Imprint (trade name)">imprint</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Academic_Press" title="Academic Press">Academic Press</a>, a subsidiary of Harcourt. Since 2001 it is an imprint of the Science and Technology division of the <a href="/wiki/Elsevier" title="Elsevier">Elsevier</a> publisher.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a
